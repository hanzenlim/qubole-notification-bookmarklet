Qubole bookmarklet

Listens to qubole api (https://api.qubole.com) for job status and alerts you when a job is done

Use Case:

A typical qubole job takes some time to finish a query and switching contexts is not unusual. With this bookmarklet, you can attach a listener to the job and will alert you when the job is done.

Usage:

After you submit a job, click the qubole bookmarklet and it will attach a listener to the job
